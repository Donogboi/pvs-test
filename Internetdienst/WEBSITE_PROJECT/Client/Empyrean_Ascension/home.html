Overview:
Empyrean Ascension is a dynamic and multifaceted company that operates across various industries, including Oil and Gas, Hospitality, Retail, and Food Services. Established in May 2023 by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Don Ogboi, the company has rapidly carved a niche for itself in diverse sectors, offering exceptional products and services to its clientele.

At the helm of Empyrean Ascension is Mr. Don Ogboi, a forward-thinking business leader with a passion for innovation and growth. With a keen understanding of market trends and an unwavering commitment to excellence, Mr. Ogboi has steered the company toward success since its inception.
